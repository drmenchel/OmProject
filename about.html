Who is this Dangerously Awesome Kid Making This Website?

And why does he do it?

My name is Om Sharma, and I love math and science. Science is the reason we are here, the reason we have things, the reason anything exists, and the reason anything ever will exist. I'm fascinated by this, and I want to learn everything about it.

When I grow up, I want to be someone who discovers, realizes, and has fun doing it. As a curious kid (which everyone calls me), I want to explore, uncover, and do something great with science.

Most people study science for money or to help civilization. For me, it's simple—I'm doing it to learn. That's it. I just want to understand how the world works.

Math is also something I enjoy, because it's the language of science. It has rules, facts, and logic—and if I get really good at math, science will be easier than anything I've ever done.
